About me

On my way to become Front-End developer! My name is Frantiska and I am originally from Czech republic. After my final Maturita exam (equivalent of the A levels in UK) I decided to leave my country and see how life goes in England. Fell madly in love with multiculture society and ended up wanting to stay! My carrier experience varies from warehouse job to administration to customer facing roles. Whatever I did I always put my 100% in! Having a few years break when staying home with my two wonderful kids to attend their every need 24/7 brought more clarity to my life and now I know I want a job that I truly enjoy! That is why I joined Front-end web development Bootcamp and I am forever grateful for this opportunity! Hopefully at the end of this course my portfolio will be filled up with beautifully looking interactive websites!!
